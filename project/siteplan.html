W05 Project: Website Planning Document Site Name Pearl of Africa Tourism This name was chosen because Uganda is known as the “Pearl of Africa” because of its beautiful scenery, wildlife, and culture. Site Purpose The purpose of this website is to provide information about tourism in Uganda. The website will help visitors learn about tourist attractions, national parks, wildlife, traditional foods, culture, and travel experiences. Scenarios What are the best tourist attractions to visit in Uganda? Which national parks are best for wildlife viewing and gorilla trekking? What traditional foods and cultural activities can tourists experience in Uganda?

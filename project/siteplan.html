Pearl of Africa Tourism - Site Plan

Site Name

Pearl of Africa Tourism

This name was chosen because Uganda is known as the “Pearl of Africa” because of its beautiful landscapes, wildlife, and culture.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide information about tourism in Uganda including tourist attractions, national parks, wildlife, traditional foods, culture, and travel experiences.

Scenarios

  • What are the best tourist attractions to visit in Uganda?
  • Which national parks are best for wildlife viewing and gorilla trekking?
  • What traditional foods and cultural activities can tourists experience?

Color Schema

  • Dark Green (#1B4332) - Header, navigation, footer, headings
  • Gold (#D4A017) - Buttons, links, highlights
  • Cream (#F8F5F0) - Background color

Typography

  • Poppins - Headings and navigation
  • Open Sans - Paragraphs and body text

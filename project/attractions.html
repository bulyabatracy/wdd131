Featured Attractions

Equator

The Uganda Equator is a famous tourist attraction located at Kayabwe in Uganda. It marks the point where the Earth is divided into the Northern and Southern Hemispheres. Tourists visit the area to take photos, watch water experiments, and buy souvenirs..

Sipi Falls

Sipi Falls is a beautiful waterfall found near Mount Elgon in eastern Uganda. The area is known for its cool climate, green scenery, and hiking activities. Many tourists visit Sipi Falls for sightseeing, photography, and nature walks..

Wildlife

Uganda is rich in wildlife and has many animals such as gorillas, lions, elephants, and giraffes. These animals are protected in national parks like Bwindi Impenetrable National Park and Murchison Falls National Park. Wildlife tourism helps to generate income and attract visitors from around the world.
